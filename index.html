About the Feast

The Girton Feast is a free annual event that brings together the community for a weekend of food, fun, and festivities. Join us for a celebration of local culture and cuisine!

Learn More

Don't Miss Our Headliner Event!

Experience an unforgettable evening with our special headliner performance. Get ready for amazing music, dazzling lights, and an atmosphere you won't forget!

Join us on Saturday night for the main event that everyone will be talking about. Limited tickets available, so book yours now!
